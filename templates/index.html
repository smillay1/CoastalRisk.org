Mission

Turn complex coastal climate data into clear, defensible guidance.

Climate conditions are worsening. Sea levels are rising, storms are intensifying, and flood zones are expanding — often faster than existing maps reflect.

At CoastalRisk, we cut through that uncertainty with property-level reports that detail the exact risks facing a specific parcel. Using NOAA sea level projections, FEMA flood classification, USGS coastal vulnerability data, and historical claims records, we help developers and investors move beyond outdated maps and generic risk scores.

Our goal is straightforward: give decision-makers the precise, sourced intelligence they need to protect assets, price risk accurately, and build with confidence in an era of accelerating coastal change.

Philosophy

Precise, confidential intelligence for those who cannot afford surprises.

Generic risk scores don’t hold up in a boardroom or a lender package. Every shoreline, every parcel, and every project has unique vulnerabilities.

We serve developers and investors who require precise, actionable intelligence on coastal exposure — not aggregated statistics, but property-specific findings grounded in the best available federal data sources.

Proactive analysis is cheaper than a surprise. A well-documented risk profile informs construction decisions, informs insurance conversations, and supports long-term asset planning in markets where climate factors are increasingly material to financing and resale.

All CoastalRisk research is independent, proprietary, and confidential.
